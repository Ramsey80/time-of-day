Time of Day

A JavaScript Project by Ramsey Mell

This is a clock for the current time and date today! The 1st line shows the day of the week it is. The 2nd line is the Time and a little greeting for you and the color also changes depending on the time of day! Also the copyright year at the very bottom should change depending on what year it currently is! And finally the image on the right should also change depending on the time of day.
